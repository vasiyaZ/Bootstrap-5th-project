University Staff Job Opportunities

INTERNAL APPLICANTS

For current UChicago employees.

Log into Workday.

From your Career Worklet, update your career profile, search and apply for UChicago staff opportunities.

How to determine you are an internal applicant:

You are able to log into Work Day. When logging into Work Day, a Career Worklet is available.

Living and working in Hyde Park

UChicago is surrounded by an engaging and exciting community that compels many to make their home in Hyde Park.With top health care facilities, outstanding schools, abundant housing choices, local retail and dining facilities, and diverse places to worship, it’s clear why many UChicago faculty and staff choose to live in Hyde Park.
